Let Freedom Ring!


Alison Lutz
July 19, 2015

Well, it's been an emotional journey for sure. Packing up my car and leaving Plattsburgh behind, I headed south toward Philly to stay with my good friend and AmeriCorps alum (and Pinterest casserole enthusiast), Kelly. We ran up the steps at the Art Museum, took the requisite Rodin "Thinker" pictures, and visited the Liberty Bell. So tourist-y... and free!

Next was DC, where I was lucky enough to stay with another one of my favorite gal pals, Erica. She and I had a blast at the Parade!

As a demonstration of the beautiful syncronicity of the universe, my visit fell on Pride Week! The city was a whirlwind of rainbows and Free Hugs.

After gorging myself on bottomless mimosas and becoming besties with Eleanor Roosevelt, it was time to pick the glitter out of my hair and leave DC for Asheville. See you next Pride, DC!
